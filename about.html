About Edify Academy

Learn more about our journey, our team, and our commitment to student success.

Our Story

Edify Academy began in 2024 with a mission to help schools find quality supply teachers. We quickly recognised the need for comprehensive educational support and evolved our services to meet the changing needs of students and schools.

In 2025, we expanded to supplying tutors directly to students, helping them access online tuition and personalised learning support. Today, we're proud to offer both online and in-person tutoring services, helping students across the UK achieve their academic goals.

Our new in-person tutoring centre opening in 2026 represents the next chapter in our journey, providing students with a dedicated space for focused learning and academic growth.

Our Journey

2024

The Beginning

We started as a company helping schools find supply teachers, connecting educational institutions with qualified professionals. Our focus was on ensuring continuity in education when regular teachers were unavailable.

2025

Expanding Services

We expanded to supplying tutors directly to students, helping them access online tuition and personalised learning support. This shift allowed us to reach more students and provide tailored educational assistance.

2026

New Chapter

We're opening our first in-person tutoring centre, providing students with a dedicated space for focused learning and academic growth. This physical space represents our commitment to accessible, quality education.

How We Teach

Our teaching methodology is designed to ensure every student receives the support they need to succeed.

01

Assessment

We begin by assessing each student's current level, identifying strengths and areas for improvement to create a personalised learning plan that targets their specific needs.

02

Personalisation

Every student receives a tailored learning experience that matches their learning style, pace, and academic goals, ensuring maximum effectiveness.

03

Engagement

Our tutors use interactive methods and real-world examples to keep students engaged and motivated throughout their learning journey.

04

Progress Tracking

Regular assessments and progress reports ensure students stay on track and parents are kept informed of their child's development.

Safeguarding

At Edify Academy, the safety and wellbeing of our students is our top priority. We are committed to providing a safe, secure, and supportive learning environment for all students.

DBS Checks

All our tutors undergo enhanced DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) checks before joining our team, ensuring the highest standards of safety.

Safeguarding Training

Every member of our team receives comprehensive safeguarding training and regular updates on best practices to protect student welfare.

Safe Learning Environment

Our tutoring centre and online platforms are designed with student safety in mind, with appropriate supervision and monitoring systems in place.

Reporting Procedures

We have clear procedures in place for reporting any concerns, ensuring students always have someone to talk to and that issues are addressed promptly.
